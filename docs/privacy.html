Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 09, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the UUID Validation and Version Detector ("we," "us," or "our"), a free client-side web utility for validating and detecting UUID versions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and operate on a zero-data-collection principle. Since all processing happens entirely in your browser, we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices to ensure transparency and trust.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any data from users, including:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses.
  • Usage Data: No tracking of browser type, device info, location, or session details.
  • Input Data: Any UUIDs you enter or upload are processed locally and never leave your device.
  • Analytics or Cookies: We use no tracking tools, cookies, or pixels.

Your data remains exclusively on your device. There are no servers involved, so nothing is logged or shared.

3. How the Tool Works

When you use our tool:

  • Local Processing: UUID validation and version detection occur in-browser using JavaScript—no external servers.
  • File Uploads: Files (.txt, .csv, .json) are read and parsed locally; nothing is uploaded.
  • Exports: Downloads (TXT, CSV, JSON) are generated on your device using browser APIs.

This ensures complete data isolation and privacy for sensitive UUIDs in logs or databases.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party services that collect data. All functionality is self-contained in the browser.

5. Data Security

While we collect no data, we recommend:

  • Using secure browsers.
  • Avoiding public devices for sensitive inputs.
  • Clearing browser cache after use.

We are not responsible for device-level security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. International Users

As no data is collected, we comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws by default.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the new effective date.

9. Contact Us

For questions, contact us at axelbase@example.com.

*Your privacy is our priority—use with confidence.*
